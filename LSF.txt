    Chap 5 Package management systems
        Intro : two main package management systems: RPM and apt
    RPM for Red Hat , fedora, oracle linux.
    APT for Debian and distributions derived from Debian.
    RPM and DPKG are low level utilities.
    Yum for Red Hat, zipper for Suse and DNF for fedora are high level package management systems
    APT (apt-get, apt-cache) high level package management system for debian.
